About Suriya

Suriya is a passionate and dedicated makeup and mehndi artist who has been in love with her craft since childhood. What started as a deep interest has now grown into a professional journey backed by over 4 years of hands-on experience.

With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to perfection, she specializes in creating elegant bridal looks and intricate mehndi designs that enhance natural beauty. Her goal is simple — to make every client feel confident, beautiful, and truly special on their important day.

Suriya believes that makeup is not just about appearance, but about bringing out the best version of every individual with grace and style.
